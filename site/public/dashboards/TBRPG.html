    O que é TBRPG?

    Um roleplay baseado em texto no qual você cria uma nova conta em qualquer plataforma de rede social e dá vida a seu personagem. Você começa a construir a história do seu personagem modificando sua conta e ingressa em comunidades dedicadas ao RPG. Este tipo de RPG é principalmente para todos que querem retratar um personagem que já existe ou até mesmo criar um novo personagem usando um rosto que já existe. Os primeiros roleplayers que usavam esse tipo de jogatina usavam contas reais ao jogar em sites SNS devido à influência dos roleplayers ocidentais. E, às vezes, usam nomes de usuário que incluam números, letras maiúsculas e minúsculas e caracteres especiais por meio da influência do sistema de interpretação de personagem.

    Tipos de Text-Based Roleplayers

    OC

    Own Character is a term for roleplayers who have their own character in RPW.
    These are mosly created from scratch and consist of different storylines/backstories.
    This is also known as PC or Player Character.
    OCs provide players with the creative freedom and the opportunity to
    bring unique and personal elementsto the game or story

    Canon

    Canonical Character is a term for roleplayers who portray a character
    from a TV show, Books, Movies and etc.
    When participating in RPW, players often adhere
    to the canon by respecting and staying true to the
    existing lore and rules established by the original creators
    This helps maintain consistency and coherence within the shared RPW universe.

    AU

    Alternative Universe is a term for roleplayers who stray from it`s original source;
    and has it's own life that is different from their usual.
    In an AU, familiar characters, settings, or events may be reimagined or placed in different
    contexts, allowing for creative exploration and storytelling possibilities.
    AU settings often involve changes to
    the timeline, character backgrounds, relationships, or the
    introduction of new elements. AU is a popular
    concept in RPW as it offers a fresh and unique approach to role-playing and storytelling.

    IC/OC Blogger

    Own/In character Blogger is a term for roleplayers who put so much detail in their captions:
    what they feel, do, etc.
    This mostly comes with a beautiful template and photos.
    It someone who maintains a blog where they both post content
    related to their in-character persona (IC) and share information or interact with others

    Comunidade de RPW:

    Uma comunidade famosa no Facebook que cresceu ao longo dos anos é chamada RPW e também pode ser encontrada em outras plataformas de rede social, como amino, discord e até twitter. Roleplaying World (RPW) se depara com a palavra “ roleplay ” que significa um ato de imitar o personagem e o comportamento de alguém que é diferente de você, “world ” significa alta sociedade, todos os tipos de RPGs se juntam a esta comunidade para socializar e interpretar com outros roleplayers. É uma comunidade para pessoas que iniciam várias linhas de diálogos e criam um mundo fictício para entretenimento.
    Em comunidades como RPW, existem regras não oficiais para os jogadores seguirem. Essas regras são uma forma de proteger os indivíduos de qualquer perigo que possa ser uma ameaça para eles na vida real. Uma vez que muitos dos novos jogadores ainda são menores de idade.

    RPW rules:

    Regra Platina

    Esconda sua verdadeira identidade.

    Regra de ouro

    Você deve se dedicar para ser seu personagem e não falar sobre nada relacionado à vida real.

    Regra de prata

    Respeite seus amigos e outros jogadores.

    RPW Abbreviations/Terms to remember:

    Terms and abbreviations allow for quick and concise communication among participants, saving time and ensuring smooth interactions during the workshop. By using standardized terms, participants can ensure that everyone is on the same page regarding concepts, techniques, and instructions. This promotes clear understanding and minimizes confusion. Terms and abbreviations create a shared vocabulary within the RPW community. This facilitates effective communication and fosters a sense of community and belonging among participants. Overall, having terms and abbreviations in RPW contributes to effective communication, shared understanding, and skill development within the role-playing community. And here are few of them you must remember:

    OP (Operator)

    Operator is a person behind the role, who controls the account. They're also known as Admins, Player or Mun.

    MUSE

    It is an another word for Character in Roleplay.

    RPGC

    It stands for Roleplaying Group Chat, where people get invited to do a group roleplay it can be closed or an open play.

    BRP

    It stands for Boy roleplayer.

    GRP

    It stands for Girl roleplayer.

    CRP

    It stands for Cross Roleplayer. These are operators who portray a character/muse that is not the same gender as they are.

    IC

    It is a term for roleplayers who are currently portraying their role/character. (In-Character)

    OOC

    It is a term for roleplayers who are currently not portraying their role/character. (Out-of-Character)

    VA

    It stands for virtual acts.

    NSFW

    Not Safe For Work is a Term for warnings about their sensitive content.

    NPC

    Non-Player Character is a non-actively played by roleplayers but are sometimes mentioned through their role.

    Crossover

    A roleplay involving two different fandoms (i.e, The vampire Diaries and The Hunger Games)
